WHAT ARE YOU FUNDING?
3.2 billion people in the 10/40 window are part of unreached people groups, making up 97% of the world’s unreached population. Shockingly, only 3% of missionaries and 1% of mission funding are dedicated to reaching these groups. We are missing the opportunity to share the love of Jesus with a vast number of people who have never heard His name. This needs to change.

The story begins in Kurdistan… Although this missions trip is for the Himalayas, it starts here.

I had the incredible opportunity to witness people being healed and saved. These weren't just small, internal healings that made people happy. I saw people healed from cancer, lifelong pain, and blindness—miracles that only Jesus can perform. I was part of this medical trip to document these miraculous events and the stories of people finding salvation in Jesus.

My role was to capture these moments on film and share the good news, showing that Jesus’s work is far from finished. He is continuing what He started.

I share this with you to highlight the importance of the mission Jesus has begun and the role He has called me to play. I will be venturing into the Himalayan mountains, where people have never heard the name of Jesus. They worship nature, selecting a new 'god' each day or continuing with the same one until they decide to change.

We will offer them hope in the One who will never fail them.

From September 16th to 30th, I, along with 17 others, will journey to the eastern and central regions of Nepal. Some of us will provide medical treatment and pray over the people, while others will travel into the mountains to minister to the villagers.

I am raising $3,000 for travel expenses and seeking your support to reach these people, breaking the statistic that only 3% of missionaries go to share the love of Jesus that make up 65% of the world’s population.

If you can’t support financially, I ask for your prayers for our team and me as we embark on this mission.
